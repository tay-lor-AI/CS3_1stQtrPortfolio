Italian Spagetti (Sponsored by DelMonte)

Rather than the sweet tomato sauce most fast food stores sell, I prefer the homecooked savory taste of my dad's spagetti!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 1/4 cup Olive Oil
  • 3/4 cup White Onion, Chopped
  • 1kg Ground Beef
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • 1 1/4 cup Cooking Sake
  • 1 pouch (1kg) DEL MONTE Italian Style Spagetti Sauce
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1/4 cup Chopped Parsley
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese

Instructions

  1. Melt salted butter in olive oil over low heat. Saut√© onion for about 5 minutes until soft.
  2. Add beef and 1 Tbsp salt. Cook until light brown. Stir in cooking sake and simmer for 3 minutes.
  3. Add DEL MONTE Italian Style Spaghetti Sauce, milk, nutmeg, and parsley (if using). Simmer for 5 minutes over low heat, stirring occasionally.
  4. Pour over cooked DEL MONTE Spaghetti. Top with parmesan cheese.
